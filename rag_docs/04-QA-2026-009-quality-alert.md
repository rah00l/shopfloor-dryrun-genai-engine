## QA-2026-009 Quality Alert, Door Hinge Bracket Flex, Model AX-500

Status: ACTIVE as of March 2026, superseded by corrective action in RCA-2026-014.

Field service has reported door hinge bracket flex under load on Model AX-500 units produced between March and May 2026 at Assembly Line 2, Station 4. Affected units may exhibit door sag or misalignment after extended use.

Immediate action: Inspect any Model AX-500 unit produced in the affected window for door hinge bracket condition before delivery. Do not release units showing visible bracket flex.

This alert will be closed once the corrective action described in RCA-2026-014 and ECN-2291 is fully implemented across the affected production window.
