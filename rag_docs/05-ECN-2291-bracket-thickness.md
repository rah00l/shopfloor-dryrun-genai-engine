## ECN-2291 Door Hinge Bracket Thickness Change

Change: Door hinge bracket thickness increased from 2mm to 2.5mm on Model AX-500, effective immediately for all new production.

Reason: Field reports of bracket flex under load, root cause identified in RCA-2026-014, originally flagged under QA-2026-009.

Affected station: Assembly Line 2, Station 4.

Impact: Installation fixture requires alignment adjustment for the new bracket thickness before installation can proceed. Torque specification is unchanged at 45 Nm (see WI-4.2). All other steps in SOP-3.1 remain unaffected.

Effective date: Immediate for all new production. Not retroactive to units already completed.
