## QA-2026-021 Quality Alert, Conveyor Belt Slippage, Assembly Line 5

Status: ACTIVE as of June 2026.

Maintenance has reported intermittent belt slippage on the Line 5 main parts conveyor during peak-load operation, occasionally causing brief part-feed delays at Station 2.

Immediate action: Increase inspection frequency for the Station 2 tensioner from weekly to daily per SOP-7.4 until root cause is identified. Report any tension readings exceeding 12mm deflection to the maintenance team lead immediately.

This alert remains active pending a root cause investigation. No corrective action has been issued yet.
