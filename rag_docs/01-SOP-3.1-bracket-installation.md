## SOP-3.1 Bracket Installation, Assembly Line 2, Station 4

This procedure covers installation of the door hinge bracket on Model AX-500 at Assembly Line 2, Station 4.

1. Confirm incoming bracket matches the current approved revision before use — check the part label against the traveler sheet.
2. Adjust the installation fixture to the alignment spec for the current bracket revision.
3. Position the bracket against the door hinge mount point, ensuring flush contact with the mounting face.
4. Torque mounting bolts to 45 Nm using a calibrated torque wrench.
5. Visually inspect for flush seating — no gap greater than 0.5mm is acceptable.
6. Log the installation under the current part revision in the station traceability sheet.

Safety note: Always wear cut-resistant gloves when handling bracket edges. Do not proceed if the fixture alignment check fails — escalate to the shift supervisor.

## SOP-3.1 Required Tools and Materials

- Calibrated torque wrench (range 20-60 Nm)
- Bracket alignment fixture (Station 4 standard)
- Cut-resistant gloves
- Station traceability log (digital or paper)
