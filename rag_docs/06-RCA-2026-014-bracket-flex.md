## RCA-2026-014 Line 2 Stoppage, Door Hinge Bracket Flex

Incident: Repeated door hinge bracket flex under load reported by field service, Model Y units produced between March and May 2026, originally flagged under QA-2026-009.

Root cause: Bracket material thickness (2mm) insufficient to prevent flex under repeated door-cycling load in high-usage fleet vehicles.

Corrective action: Bracket thickness increased to 2.5mm per ECN-2291. Fixture alignment updated to accommodate new bracket dimensions. See SOP-3.1 (Rev C) for the updated installation procedure.

Status: Closed. Verified effective after a 30-day field monitoring period, no repeat reports.

## RCA-2026-014 Contributing Factors

- Original bracket spec (2mm) had not been re-validated since Model Y's last major design refresh, despite an increase in average fleet duty cycle.
- No automated stress-test flag existed for bracket thickness relative to updated duty-cycle assumptions.
- Recommend: add duty-cycle-linked spec review as a standing check for future model refreshes.
