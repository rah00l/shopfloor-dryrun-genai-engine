## MM-Station4 Fixture Preventive Maintenance Schedule

The bracket alignment fixture at Assembly Line 2, Station 4 requires the following preventive maintenance:

- Daily: Visual inspection for visible wear or misalignment before first use of the shift.
- Weekly: Alignment recalibration check using the standard gauge block set.
- Monthly: Full fixture teardown, clean, and lubrication of moving components.
- Quarterly: Third-party calibration certification renewal.

## MM-Station4 Fixture Troubleshooting

If bracket seating shows a gap greater than 0.5mm during installation despite following SOP-3.1:

1. Check the fixture's last calibration date — if overdue, remove from service immediately.
2. Inspect the fixture's locating pins for wear or damage.
3. If pins are worn, replace before further use — do not attempt field repair.
4. If the issue persists after pin replacement, escalate to the maintenance team lead for full fixture inspection.
